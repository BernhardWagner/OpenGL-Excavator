This is just a small project playing around with openGL creating an excavator.

press h to get help for the inputs.




Press the right Mouse button and move it to rotate the scene.

KEYBOARD INPUTS:
[<-] and [->]                    Rotates the cabin
[UP] and [DOWN]                  Moves the excavator in one direction
[y] and [x]                      Rotation of the first excavator-arm-part
[c] and [v]                      Rotation of the second excavator-arm-part
[b] and [n]                      Rotation of the third excavator-arm-part
[m] and [,]                      Rotation of the multi-part-bucket
[SPACE]                          Start/Stop Animation
[1][2][3][4][5]                  Change the velocity of the animation
[h]                              Prints the helping menu
[RIGHT MOUSEBUTTON]     Rotates the entire Scene
